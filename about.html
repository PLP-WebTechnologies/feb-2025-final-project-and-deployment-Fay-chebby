Hello there! I'm Faith Chebet, the writer behind Simple Scribbles. Welcome to my corner of the internet where I share my thoughts, experiences, and occasional randomly.

My Story

I started this blog in 2025 as a way to document my journey as a web developer and creative writer. Having worked in the tech industry for over 5 years, I found myself constantly learning new things and wanting a place to share these insights.

Outside of coding, I'm passionate about literature, hiking, photography, and trying to keep my houseplants alive (with varying degrees of success). I believe in lifelong learning and hope that my writing reflects this curiosity about the world.
